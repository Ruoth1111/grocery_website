About GrosFresh

Learn more about our story, mission, and the team behind your favorite grocery delivery service

Our Story

GrosFresh was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make fresh, high-quality groceries accessible to everyone. What started as a small local delivery service in downtown San Francisco has grown into a nationwide operation serving millions of happy customers.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, was frustrated with the limited selection of fresh produce in her neighborhood grocery stores and the time it took to shop for quality ingredients. She believed there had to be a better way to connect local farmers and food producers directly with consumers.

From those humble beginnings, GrosFresh has revolutionized how people shop for groceries, emphasizing sustainability, supporting local economies, and making healthy eating convenient for busy families.

Our Mission

At GrosFresh, we're committed to transforming the grocery shopping experience while promoting sustainable food systems and supporting local communities.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly packaging, reduce food waste, and work with farmers who use sustainable growing practices.

Convenience

We make healthy eating easy with user-friendly ordering and flexible delivery options that fit into your busy lifestyle.
