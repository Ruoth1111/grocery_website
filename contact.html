Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all major cities and surrounding areas in the United States. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your zip code on our delivery page or during checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery in real-time through our website or app.

What is your return policy?

We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with any product, simply contact us within 24 hours of delivery, and we'll process a refund or replacement for you.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Click on the user icon in the top navigation bar, then select "Sign Up." Fill in your details, and you'll be ready to shop in minutes.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery in select areas for orders placed before 11 AM. You can check if same-day delivery is available for your location during checkout.
